About Us

Welcome to Maithil Mosaic, your destination for the rich and authentic flavors of Mithila!

Rooted in the vibrant culture of Bihar and Nepal, Mithila cuisine is a harmonious blend of tradition, artistry, and unforgettable taste. We proudly serve dishes crafted with love and a deep respect for authenticity, ensuring you experience the true essence of this cherished culinary heritage.

Delight in the rustic charm of Litti Chokha, the sweet indulgence of Pedakiya and Laung Latika, and the savory goodness of Chana Gughni, Dal Pitha, and Mutton Kabab. Enjoy the smokey crunch of Bhooja or the rich, aromatic flavors of Rohu Fish Curry—each dish tells a story of Mithila’s culinary traditions.

Our passion lies in preserving and sharing these treasured recipes, passed down through generations. We aim to bring you the warmth of a traditional Mithila kitchen combined with the convenience of modern dining. Whether you’re rediscovering the tastes of home or exploring this unique cuisine for the first time, Maithil Mosaic promises a flavorful journey that satisfies every palate.

Join us as we celebrate the flavors, culture, and soul of Mithila with every bite.

Savor tradition. Experience Mithila.
